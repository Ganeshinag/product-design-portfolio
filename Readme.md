## 🚀 Featured Projects from Portfolio

### 🩺 Electro-Mechanical Medical Device Development (Digital Stethoscope Project)

As part of my current role, I contribute to the mechanical design and development of a compact electro-mechanical medical device intended for enhanced auscultation and remote patient monitoring.

My work focuses on translating functional requirements from electronics, sensing, and testing teams into reliable, manufacturable mechanical solutions.

**My engineering involvement includes:**

- Designing tolerance-controlled assemblies to accommodate PCB, MEMS sensors, microphones, and micro-components within a compact enclosure
- Developing standardized 2D manufacturing drawings with GD&T to improve manufacturing clarity and reduce interpretation errors
- Performing part-level fit validation and tolerance analysis for electro-mechanical integration
- Iterative SLA/FDM prototyping to validate assembly feasibility, structural behavior, and thermal considerations
- Applying DFMA principles for CNC machining, acrylic fabrication, and injection-molded parts with vendor coordination
- Supporting design verification through prototype testing, performance tracking, and corrective design improvements
- Improving CAD and drawing release workflow to reduce rework and increase documentation reliability
- Working closely with electrical, hardware test, and manufacturing teams to align mechanical solutions with system requirements

> This project reflects my approach to electro-mechanical product development, where CAD precision, manufacturability, and validation are critical to successful product realization.
